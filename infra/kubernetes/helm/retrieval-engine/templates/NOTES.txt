1. Get service URLs:

  minikube service list -p retrieval

  Query:     minikube service {{ include "retrieval-engine.fullname" . }}-query -p retrieval --url
  Itinerary: minikube service {{ include "retrieval-engine.fullname" . }}-itinerary -p retrieval --url
  Jaeger UI: minikube service {{ include "retrieval-engine.fullname" . }}-jaeger -p retrieval --url

2. Or port-forward (stable localhost ports):

  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "retrieval-engine.fullname" . }}-query 8000:8000
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "retrieval-engine.fullname" . }}-itinerary 8002:8002
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "retrieval-engine.fullname" . }}-jaeger 16686:16686

3. Enqueue ingestion work:

  kubectl exec deploy/{{ include "retrieval-engine.fullname" . }}-worker -- \
    enqueue-job pipeline --limit 5000

4. Load test (requires k6 + corpus loaded):

  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "retrieval-engine.fullname" . }}-query 8000:8000
  BASE_URL=http://localhost:8000 k6 run tests/load/search.js
